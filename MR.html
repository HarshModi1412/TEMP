Meditation Resources To Improve Your Mental Health

March 22, 2023

Headspace - a popular meditation app that offers guided meditations, mindfulness exercises, and other resources for meditation and relaxation.

Calm - another meditation app that features guided meditations, sleep stories, and other tools for relaxation and stress relief.

Insight Timer - a free meditation app that offers a large library of guided meditations, music, and talks from leading meditation teachers.

UCLA Mindful Awareness Research Center - offers free guided meditations in English and Spanish, along with other resources for mindfulness and meditation.

Tara Brach - a meditation teacher and author who offers guided meditations, talks, and courses on mindfulness and self-compassion.

The Chopra Center - founded by Deepak Chopra, this center offers a variety of resources for meditation, yoga, and holistic health.

Mindful - a website and magazine that provides articles, guided meditations, and other resources on mindfulness and meditation.

Zen Habits - a popular blog that offers articles and resources on mindfulness, meditation, and simplicity.

The Mindfulness Summit - an annual online event featuring talks and guided meditations from leading mindfulness and meditation teachers.

Dharma Seed - a website that offers free audio and video recordings of talks and guided meditations from Buddhist teachers.
